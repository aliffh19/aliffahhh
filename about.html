About me

My name is Aliffah Ikramiena, and I'm a 21-year-old!

I was born in Kajang, Selangor, but I currently live in Penang. My hometown is in Kelantan, and I've been living in Penang for almost 12 years. It has been quite fun, but if you ask me more deeply, I love staying in Kelantan or Terengganu because I enjoy the vibes of my hometown.

My interest in creating websites and editing designs stems from the joy I feel when my designs and websites are successful. Since I am studying in the field of Information Management, I aspire to become a successful web developer one day, and I also dream of becoming a lecturer.

I come from a small family but have many cats. I have six cats named Baby, Leo, Maya, Oyen, Adik, and Labu! I love them dearly and consider them like my siblings, as I am the youngest child with one brother and two sisters.

Nasi Kerabu holds a special place in my heart because it’s not just a meal; it’s a taste of my Kelantanese roots. My favorite travel destination

Semporna, Sabah

Semporna, Sabah, is my favorite travel destination because it offers breathtaking views and unforgettable experiences. Known for its stunning islands and crystal-clear waters, Semporna is a paradise for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. This beautiful destination is at the top of my list of places to visit, as I am eager to explore its vibrant marine life and immerse myself in the local culture. The serene beaches and captivating sunsets make Semporna an ideal getaway for relaxation and adventure.
